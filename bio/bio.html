Biography & Experience.

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio website. My name is Krista Velasco & i'd like to tell you a little about myself and my journey. I graduated studying culinary arts at “Culinary Art School” in Tijuana B.C. And worked in several restaurants in the area while studying. In 2012, I partnered to open “Cerveceria Wendlandt” in Downtown Ensenada B.C. Recognized today as the best brewery in Mexico. My dishes at “Cerveceria Wendlandt” reflects the life style of Baja California, using only local and fresh ingredients made by hand. All the preparations were handmade from the bread to the tostadas to the salsas and the aioli.


In 2013, “Cerveceria Wendlandt” expanded, opening a new concept- “Troika” in collaboration with the restaurant “Corazon de Tierra” and the winery “Vena Cava”. Between Chef Diego Hernandez and I, we created the menu for “Troika”. I was the head chef for a year and a half and I trained all the cooks in order to leave everything running to the highest of standards, while also managing my kitchen in “Cerveceria Wendlandt”.


In 2015, I partnered with young entrepreneurs from San Luis Rio Colorado to develop “La Faisana”. A gastro pub, which works with all locally sourced products from the region. I was in charge of creating the menu and training all of the crew in the kitchen. I still work with my partners, changing the menu twice a year as well as keeping up with training for the kitchen crew.


In 2017, “La faisana” opened in Mexicali BC. In “Puerco Salvaje”, which is a local brewery. I created a completely new menu for them, making sure to educate myself about all of the local products that are available as well as pairing dishes with the house beer.


In 2018, I launched my own brand “K’s Handmade” which is a variety of handmade products, such as sauces, preserves, pickled veggies, fresh bread and much more. All made with local, fresh products from the Baja California region.

I now work on private events, my handmade products and also collaborate with chefs of the region. I have appeared in several media outlets, most recently in Lonely Plant food, “Mexico from the source”. I am 100% bilingual, always on time, enjoy working as part of a team or as a team leader. I love to learn as well as teach. Cooking is my passion! Having the ability to share my love for the product, to transform it and most of all see how food units people and feeds the soul.
